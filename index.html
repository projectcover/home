Capturing the Details

A Passion Project

This collection isn't just a portfolio; it's the start of a journey. As a photography enthusiast with a keen eye for product design, I've always admired brands that merge form and function seamlessly. I personally purchased and photographed each case in this collection to capture the unique story each one tells.

This project was born from a genuine appreciation for great design and a desire to bring products to life through the lens. It represents my first step into professional collaboration, driven by passion and a commitment to quality. I believe the best partnerships come from a place of mutual respect for the craft.

This inaugural collection focuses on the Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra, with the S25 Ultra series coming next.

S23 Ultra Case Collection

11zeros - Corrugated

A bold design with a textured, industrial feel.

The Industrial Aesthetic

The 11zeros Corrugated case isn't just about protection; it's about tactile feedback. Inspired by shipping containers and industrial architecture, the back panel features deep, 3D corrugated ridges that catch the light and create distinct shadows.

These ridges provide an unmatched grip, ensuring the massive S23 Ultra stays firmly in your hand. The contrasting orange camera ring adds a streetwear aesthetic that pops against the matte black finish, making it a statement piece.

Tech Specs

  • Material Polycarbonate (Hard)
  • Texture 3D Corrugated Ridges
  • Style Industrial w/ Orange Accent

Key Features

  • Tactile Grip: The 3D ridges prevent slips.
  • Visual Contrast: The neon orange accent ring draws the eye to the camera array.
  • Slim Profile: Despite the texture, it remains pocket-friendly.

Caseology - Nano Pop

Minimalist and sleek with a pop of color.

Bold & Minimal

The Caseology Nano Pop is inspired by the dual-color visuals of fresh fruit. It strikes a balance between playfulness and professionalism. The silicone feel is soft to the touch but attracts less lint than traditional silicone cases.

The yellow "Pop" around the camera module highlights the S23 Ultra's most powerful feature, while the "Black Sesame" body keeps the overall look grounded.

Tech Specs

  • Material Silicone / TPU
  • Texture Soft-Touch Matte
  • Style Two-Tone Minimalist

Design Highlights

  • Soft-Touch Finish: Feels premium in the hand.
  • Internal Protection: Patterned interior for shock dispersion.
  • Raised Bezels: Protects the screen and cameras from flat surfaces.

Spigen - Liquid Air

A form-fitted case with enhanced grip.

The Modern Classic

Spigen's Liquid Air has become a staple for a reason. It features a signature geometric pattern engraved into the back that serves two purposes: it repels fingerprints and provides a secure, non-slip grip.

Despite being incredibly thin, it utilizes Spigen's "Air Cushion Technology" in the corners to absorb shock from drops, making it the perfect daily driver for those who hate bulky cases.

Tech Specs

  • Material Flexible TPU
  • Texture Geometric Diamond Grip
  • Style Slim Profile

Technical Details

  • Air Cushion Tech: Military-grade drop protection.
  • Geometric Pattern: Matte finish that stays clean.
  • Button Response: Clicky and responsive feedback.

Casesily - Carbon Fiber

Sophisticated look with a racing stripe.

High-End Materiality

This case utilizes Aramid Fiber (often referred to as Kevlar), a material used in aerospace and body armor. It is exceptionally strong yet lighter than plastic. The weave pattern reflects light in a unique way, giving it a premium sheen.

The "Racing Stripe" down the center breaks up the pattern, adding a sense of speed and motion to the design. It is incredibly thin, almost feeling like part of the phone itself.

Tech Specs

  • Material Aramid Fiber
  • Texture Smooth Woven Feel
  • Style Racing Stripe Pattern

Material Benefits

  • Ultra-Thin: 0.6mm thickness.
  • Scratch Resistant: High durability against keys and coins.
  • Heat Dissipation: Breathable material helps phone cooling.
